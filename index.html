About the Association

We collaborate with local advocates, nutritionists, farmers, and policymakers to expand access to ethical, plant-centered food systems across the Caucasus region.

Programs & Initiatives

Our core programs include school outreach, culinary training for restaurants, farm partnerships, and public education campaigns that highlight the benefits of plant-forward living.

Events & Gatherings

Seasonal markets, urban garden tours, film screenings, and volunteer meetups help us build supportive networks and celebrate sustainable food traditions.

Restaurants

Explore our trusted network of plant-forward dining partners across the region.

Recipes & Nutrition

We share culturally diverse meal plans, chef-developed recipes, and nutrition tips for every stage of life.

Membership & Volunteers

Members receive advocacy updates, training opportunities, and resources to lead local initiatives. Join a volunteer circle or start a chapter in your city.

Contact & Partnerships

Connect with us to collaborate on community programs, request media interviews, or become a sponsoring partner.
